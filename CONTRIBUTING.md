# Contributing

Thanks for contributing to the Card Madness Community Packs repository!   
This repo is **community-driven** and focuses on **card pack content** (not the game engine).

## Quick rules

- All packs **must** be licensed under **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0**.
- Only submit content you **own** or have **permission** to redistribute under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.
- **No commercial use**: packs may not be sold or used to generate profit.
- Keep it respectful and original. No hate speech, harassment, or illegal content.
